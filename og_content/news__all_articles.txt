# 2024 European Battery Exhibition, jointly drawing a green future!

Published: 6/22/2024

LNPE, a powder equipment manufacturer with research and development as its main focus, made a brilliant debut at the 2024 European Battery Exhibition held at the Stuttgart Convention and Exhibition Center in Germany recently. This not only significantly improves the consistency and purity of the materials, but also effectively reduces energy consumption and emissions, providing solid technical support for the green upgrading of the battery industry chain.

In addition, LNPE has also demonstrated its intelligent production line solution. Combining advanced Internet of Things technology and big data analysis to achieve full process monitoring and optimization from raw material processing to finished product packaging, greatly improving production efficiency and product quality. Through close cooperation with global customers, the company is gradually building an open and shared powder technology ecosystem, working together to promote the sustainable development of the global new energy industry.

With the successful conclusion of the 2024 European Battery Exhibition, the outstanding performance of LNPE undoubtedly adds a significant contribution to the prosperity and development of the global new energy industry. Also, it indicates that the influence of Chinese enterprises in the field of new energy is increasing day by day, steadily moving towards becoming a global leader in energy solutions.

# LNPE was invited to participate in the first 2024 "China Powder Industry Hundred People Forum" and publish a key technology research report

Published: 6/13/2024

From June 11th to June 13th, 2024, at the inaugural "China Powder Industry Hundred People Forum" held in Guangzhou, Dr. Chen Jundong from LNPE was invited to participate and presented a report on "Key Technologies in Preparation of New Energy Battery Materials Powders." His presentation highlighted innovative applications of steam kinetic jet mill and controlled milling technologies.

The forum, co-initiated by the Powder Technology Branch of the China Electronic Materials Industry Association and the Powder Circle Platform, aimed to gather experts and entrepreneurs in the field of powder materials from domestic and international arenas to discuss the future of Chinese powder industry. The theme of the forum was "Powder Technology Accelerating Chinese Manufacturing Industry," covering topics such as the integration of powder industry into the national economy, new energy sectors, and aspects of safety and environmental protection.

During the forum, Dr. Chen Jundong elaborated on steam kinetic jet mill and controlled milling technologies. Steam kinetic jet mill utilizes superheated steam as a grinding power, converting thermal energy into high-quality kinetic energy. It delivers high-intensity, high-efficiency grinding effects and efficient drying capabilities simultaneously. For instance, in grinding calcined coke, a single machine can achieve a production rate of 5t/h (d50=7μm). When used for grinding lithium carbonate with 10% moisture content, it can dry the material to as low as 0.02% moisture content during grinding. This technology demonstrates outstanding performance in grinding and drying materials such as calcined coke and lithium carbonate.

Additionally, Dr. Chen introduced controlled milling technology. This technology employs a uniquely developed airflow control structure to achieve controllable and adjustable grinding intensity, resulting in products with high yield and economic efficiency. For example, when used for post-sintering ternary material grinding, it achieves a 100% yield without any raw material loss, significantly enhancing raw material utilization rates. Due to its controllable grinding intensity, it can also be used for disaggregation of agglomerated powder materials, achieving disaggregation without loss. This technology exhibits significant advantages in areas such as ternary material grinding and disaggregation of agglomerated powder materials, greatly improving raw material utilization rates.

Dr. Chen Jundong's presentation representing LNPE received high praise from experts and entrepreneurs alike at the forum. It was unanimously agreed that the innovative applications of these two technologies will inject new vitality into Chinese powder industry and drive the transformation and upgrading of manufacturing.

LNPE, as a leading domestic manufacturer of powder equipment, has been committed to the research and application of powder technology. Their participation in the "China Powder Industry Hundred People Forum" and the presentation of key technological research not only showcased the company's technical strength and innovative achievements but also provided more opportunities and challenges for the future development of LNPE.

Looking ahead, LNPE will continue to advance the progress and application of powder technology, contributing to the accelerated development of Chinese manufacturing industry.

# Participated in the first "China Powder Industry Hundred People Forum" and published a key technology research report

Published: 6/13/2024

From June 11 to 13, 2024, Guangzhou held the first "China Powder Industry Hundred People Forum", jointly initiated by the Powder Technology Branch of the China Electronic Materials Industry Association and the Powder Circle Platform. The forum focused on "Powder Technology Accelerates China's Manufacturing Industry", involving topics such as the integration of the powder industry with the national economy and the new energy industry chain, as well as safe production and environmental protection.

Dr. Chen Jundong of Mianyang Liuneng was invited to attend the meeting and delivered a report on "Research on Key Technologies for the Preparation of New Energy Battery Material Powders". He focused on the technologies pioneered by Mianyang Liuneng and implemented on a large scale in industrialization: steam kinetic mill and controlled crushing technology. The steam kinetic mill uses superheated steam as the crushing power to achieve efficient crushing and drying functions. For example, in the crushing of calcined coke, the single machine output reaches 5t/h (d50=7μm), and can dry lithium carbonate with a water content of 10% to 0.02% moisture. The controlled pulverization technology uses a unique airflow control structure to achieve controllable adjustment of the pulverization intensity. The yield rate of ternary materials after sintering is 100%, and it can be used for non-destructive depolymerization of agglomerated powder materials. These technological innovations will add new impetus to the development of China's powder industry.
