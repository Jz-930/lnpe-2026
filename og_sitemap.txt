- 标题: `Jet Mill`

  - `Working Principle`
  - 多段正文说明:
    - 多喷嘴 sonic-speed airflow
    - 颗粒间碰撞实现超细粉碎
    - 高效 turbine classifier 分离粗细颗粒
    - 强调理论建模、数值模拟、国内外技术整合
    - 突出 low energy consumption、minimal over-grinding、narrow PSD、易操作
    - 提供 low-temperature、high-temperature、inert gas closed-loop 等方案
  - `Feature`
    - Low energy consumption
    - Materials collision each other without contamination, effective for Mohs 1-10 hard Material
    - Fully automatic control, easy to operate
    - Omniseal negative pressure operation, no contamination.

2. `/products/impact-mill`
- 标题: `Impact Mill`
- 板块:
  - 正文说明:
    - high-speed hammer impact + hammer-to-liner compression
    - built-in turbine classifier
    - 适用于 adhesive、fibrous、heat-sensitive 等物料
    - 强调 compact、high grinding efficiency、low specific energy consumption、narrow PSD
  - `Feature`
    - High-capacity throughput with exceptionally low specific energy consumption
    - Optional full-ceramic process lining to virtually eliminate ferromagnetic contamination

3. `/products/jet-pulverizer`
- 标题: `Jet Pulverizer`
- 板块:
  - `Working Principle`
  - 正文说明:
    - 是 patented next-generation grinding-and-classification system
    - `Immediate Classification`
    - `Single-Collision Milling`
    - `Ultra-Short Residence Time`
    - 强调 stable particle sizes、high yields、energy efficiency
  - `Key Application Areas`
    - Sintered ternary cathode powders
    - De-agglomeration and micronization of sintered silicon-carbon composites
    - De-agglomeration and dispersion of coated graphite anode materials
    - Reflective and high-purity silicon powders
    - Precision milling of lithium-ion battery cathode and anode constituents
  - `Feature`
    - Controlled grinding, high yield, stable particle size, low energy consumption, and large output
    - The milling strength is easy to control, high yield, low fine powder content
    - Minimal impact on particle surface morphology, with little damage to the shape of particles

5. `/products/shaping-mill`
- 标题: `Shaping Mill`
- 板块:

  - `Typical Applications`
    - Spheroidisation of natural and synthetic graphite
    - Surface smoothing of mica and talc

6. `/products/air-classifier`
- 标题: `Air Classifier`
- 板块:
  - 原理说明:
    - 按 particle size 与 density 做干法分级
    - 通过 centrifugal force 与 drag force 平衡实现分离
    - 大颗粒/重颗粒走 coarse-product outlet
    - 小颗粒/轻颗粒进入 fine-product stream
  - `Feature`
    - Precision separation by particle size, density, and morphology
    - CFD-optimised airflow, solids concentration, and pressure profile for each design
    - High cut accuracy with classification efficiency of 70-93%
    - Multiple interchangeable classifier-wheel geometries
    - Typical applications: lithium-battery cathodes, anodes, separators, and other advanced powder materials

七、重构时值得注意的异常与潜在遗漏点

1. 已明确发现的站内问题
- Footer About 链接写成 `/aboout`，会 404。
- Header 左上角品牌文字仍是 `Your Company`，像是占位文案未替换。
- `7×24 Standby Professionally` 模块第 3、4 张卡标题重复为 `Product Selection`。
- `Book a Demostration Trail or Visit` 文案存在拼写错误。
- 多个页面文案存在零散拼写/大小写问题，例如:
  - `Spring is approching`
  - `LNE provides ...`（少了 P）
  - `ally@Inpe.com.cn`（域名前缀大小写与品牌不一致）

2. SEO/技术层面的共性问题
- 全站 `<title>` 几乎没有区分页面类型，全部为 `LNPE`。
- 全站 description 几乎没有区分页面类型，均为 `LNPE Powder Technology`。
- 未发现 `robots.txt` 与 `sitemap.xml`。

3. 内容结构上的结论
- 首页负责品牌总览 + 产品入口 + 研发实力 + 服务承诺。
- `/products` 是设备总目录；`/products/*` 为标准化产品详情模板。
- `/applications` 只有材料/行业方向，没有拆出单独应用详情页。
- `/projects` 有 6 个项目入口，但项目详情页基本是“标题 + 日期 + 图片案例”，正文很少。
- `/news` 是更新最频繁的栏目，详情页内容最完整。
- `/contact-us` 是唯一有表单提交逻辑的页面。

4. 若后续要做重构/迁移，建议优先保留的信息资产
- 产品体系: 7 个产品分类与各自核心卖点
- 应用体系: 19 个材料方向
- 项目体系: 6 个案例入口与对应图集
- 新闻体系: 16 篇新闻详情
- 联系体系: 北美办公室、中国工厂、国际业务联系方式
- 服务体系: `7×24 Standby Professionally` 与 `Material Trial & Industry Tour`
